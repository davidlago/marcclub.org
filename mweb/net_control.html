MARC On-Air Nets

MARC conducts an on-air net every Sunday evening at 7:30 pm on our 146.955 repeater.
All hams are encouraged to check into the Sunday net.

MARC conducts an on-air net every Tuesday evening at 8:00 pm on our 146.955 repeater.
All hams interested in Public Service and Emergency Communications (Emcomm)
are welcome to check into the Tuesday net.

In addition to MARC Nets, there are more local DC/MD/VA nets ...


Would you like to have a try at being a net control operator?

All Club members are welcome to run the Sunday night net. The net is friendly and easy-going,
so it's a good place to get your feet wet as a net control operator. To assist control operators,
example scripts for the Sunday night net and the Tuesday night net are available online.
