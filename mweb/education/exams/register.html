Registering for a volunteer examiner test session

 

The Montgomery Amateur Radio Club volunteer examiner public exam sessions are usually held in the Science Center building of Montgomery College, Rockville campus.  On Saturdays, you may park in any spot in the parking lot next to the Science Center building other than in handicap parking or in a fire lane.

To register for a test, you will need to fill out a NCVEC QUICK-FORM 605 APPLICATION form with your FCC Registration Number (FRN).  If you do not have an FRN, then you need to obtain one by creating an account with the Federal Communications Commission (FCC).  Be sure to apply as an individual and not as a business.  The FRN obscures your social security number (SSN) since we ham radio volunteers do not want to see your SSN.  But, more importantly, it reduces typing errors caused by volunteers who type in your information and speeds up processing of your license application.  Also, please provide a valid personal email address since that is how we can notify you of your new license.

Use Adobe Acrobat Reader to fill out Section 1 of the Form 605.  After you fill out the form, use Save As... in the File menu to save your filled out Form 605.  Be sure to use your name as part of the file name of your saved Form 605.  Look at a sample filled out Form 605 for obtaining a new or a sample filled out Form 605 for upgrading your license as appropriate.  Please be sure to provide a valid personal email on the form so we can contact you if there is a problem.  Do not sign your Form 605 since you and three volunteer examiners will sign it when you pass the one or more exams that you may have taken.

Then email your filled out Form 605 to education@marcclub.org, print it and bring it with you to the exam session.  Also, bring a government issued photo ID such as a drivers license to show at the test session.  The exam is free since we are a Laurel VEC team.  Please do not hesitate to email us with any questions you may have.
