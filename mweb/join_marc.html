Thank you for your interest in MARC


MARC is a non-profit, tax exempt, 501(c)(3) organization which means dues and most donations
are tax deductible.

You may join MARC or renew your membership online and pay with PayPal or a credit card (a PayPal account is NOT needed to pay via credit card).

If you prefer to pay by check, please complete the online form and send a copy of the "invoice" the online system generates along with your payment to the club's mailing address:

Montgomery Amateur Radio Club
P.O. Box 611
Gaithersburg, MD 20884-0611

If you are unable to use the online process, you may download the MARC membership form, fill it out, and mail the form along with your payment to the club at the above address.

You do not have to be a member of MARC to attend meetings or to use the MARC repeater.

We do, however, invite you to join us by simply filling out an application form and paying the proper membership dues shown on the form at the time of application. This can be done at any meeting or mail your application and dues to:


MARC
P.O. Box 611
Gaithersburg, MD 20884-0611


Your application must be presented by the membership committee to the membership at two meetings (usually the two immediately following receipt of the application) to be voted into the club. Upon becoming a member, you will receive a Directory of MARC members and a membership card.
